Pop — Course Tasting

Need some advice?

Find out who else took a course you're interested in.

It's easy:

  1. You share the courses you've took. It's only fair if the person you're asking for advice can get advice from you too.
  2. You get to see other people who've taken the course you're interested in, as well as their contact info to get in touch.
  3. Get to ask them questions about the course! They might have some questions for you, or maybe another person will.

To get started, follow the steps below.

Step 1: Import your courses

We know you don't want to type out all the courses you've taken, so we're making it easier.

Note: You must be doing this on a laptop or desktop - mobile browsers don't support copying source code.

  1. Login to MyIIT and open the Academics tab.
  2. In the Banner Self-Service panel, follow the navigation to get to the Registration History link. See the included image for how to do that.
  3. Right click near the Registration History heading and select "View frame source" (or "This Frame > View frame source" on Firefox).
  4. A new tab should open with some code. Select it all with Ctrl+A and then copy with Ctrl+C.
  5. Paste with Ctrl+V into the big text area below.
  6. Click the big "Import" button and we will automatically parse the code to get a list of all the courses you've taken. Don't worry, we don't save the contents of what you paste so your grades and other info remain private.

Step 2: Fill out the rest of the form

After we've successfully imported your courses, you will be redirected to a form where you will need to fill in two additional pieces of info - your name and email. That way we can grant you access to our master document.

Step 3: Search and ask away

Once you filled out the form, you will be granted access to a Google Spreadsheet with everyone's course list and contact info, so you can ask as many people about as many courses as you want. (and they can do the same to you)
